My Favorite Recipe

A singular, slightly seasoned egg

  • One egg (raw, ideally)
  • One pan
  • Irish butter
  • Salt
  • Garlic powder

  • Grab the egg out of your fridge
  • Grab a pan, from wherever you may store it
  • Turn on stove and add butter into the pan
  • Once butter is melted, crack the egg into the pan (best results from cracking egg exactly 3 inches above the pan)
  • Wait for the egg to sizzle and bubble
  • Flip the egg and wait again
  • Optional: Add a dash of salt. Perhaps a modest amount of garlic powder
  • Enjoy!
